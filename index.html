Marketing Campaign Analysis

A comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was conducted encompassing both numerical and categorical variables, with a focus on optimizing data for segmentation by addressing missing values and scaling the dataset. Advanced dimensionality reduction techniques, including Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and T-Distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding (T-SNE), were employed to alleviate multicollinearity and prepare the dataset for clustering algorithms. Various clustering algorithms such as K-Means, K-Medoids, Hierarchical Clustering, DBSCAN, and Gaussian Mixture Models were implemented to discern the optimal model. Following the utilization of clearly defined groups as a benchmark, it was determined that K-Medoids emerged as the most suitable model for the dataset.
